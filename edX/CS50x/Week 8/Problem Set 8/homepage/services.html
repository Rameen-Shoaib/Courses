Consultation and Diagnosis

We offers a very clean treatment and proper examination of your oral health. For proper diagnosis, Our clinic properly access your oral hygiene, examine tooth decay, evaluate dental caries' risks, and properly inspect the need to replace any previous crown or dental implants.

Root Canal Treatment

This is an advanced endodontic dental treatment where the dentist saves an abscessed or a painfully decayed tooth by anesthetizing it and cleaning the root canals of the teeth. The dentist then makes a customized crown and place over the treated tooth for a beautiful smile and cosmetics purposes.
